    ITINERARY

    INFORMATION

    This amazing tour of Toronto begins with a pick-up directly from your hotel. As soon as our guide picks you up, you are taken to the majestic Toronto Tower. At the tower, you will be whisked upwards in the transparent elevation structure to the observation areas. From here, you can take in panoramic views of the entire city. You will also get the opportunity to feel the refreshing breeze on the beautiful outdoor terrace.


    The next stop is Casa Loma. Here, you will be provided with a narrated tour of the entire place. At the same time, on your way to the castle, you will be given information about various landmarks including Nathan Phillips Square, Queen’s Park, and Dundas Square. Once you reach Nathan Phillips Square, you can also take a picture with the famous Toronto sign while learning about the PATH underneath the city.


    You will then be escorted to Casa Loma – the only full-sized castle in North America. It is a majestic and mysterious castle featuring multiple secret passages, turrets, and tunnels. From May to October, the castle is known to feature 4 acres of beautiful gardens that surround the entire property and overlook the beautiful city of Toronto.


    The last activity of the tour is a Toronto Harbour Cruise boat tour (available on May-October only, during wintertime, it is replaced by a visit to the Distillery Market) which will lead you to the beautiful views of the Toronto skyline and islands around the city!

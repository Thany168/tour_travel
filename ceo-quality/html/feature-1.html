    ITINERARY

    INFORMATION

    The popular tour highlights the best ways to experience the unmatched beauty of the world-famous Niagara Falls. Onboard a majestic Niagara Falls cruise boat, you will experience the massive falls up close. You’ll even venture behind and underneath the powerful water with the help of the Journey Behind the Falls. If that’s not enough, you’ll also get to admire the majestic beauty of this one-of-a-kind attraction from above in the Skylon Tower.


    The fun-filled and thrilling tour of the mesmerizing Niagara Falls commences at your hotel where you will be picked up. The first stop on the tour is Skylon Tower. You will head up the breathtaking glass elevator to the observation deck. Here, you will marvel at the amazing, bird’s-eye view of the Niagara Falls. You will have a panoramic view of the three Niagara Falls including American Falls, Horseshoe Falls, and Bridal Veil Falls. At the same time, you also get to enjoy the views of the underlying Niagara River and take in the area’s immense natural beauty.


    Once you’re done taking in the views, you will get the opportunity to descend around 40 meters to explore the cave-like tunnels located behind the falls at Journey Behind the Falls. This experience will be a treat for all of your senses. This is because , you not only get to hear the thunderous sound of the falls, but you will also get an opportunity to feel its immense power. The tunnels lead to two beautiful observation decks, offering you the perfect opportunity to take excellent photos.


    Once you have visited the spectacular Journey Behind the Falls, you will be taken on the Niagara cruise boat journey. On the boat, you get to traverse across the waters of the Niagara River while getting as close to the falls as possible. You can feel the refreshing water as well the mist. To protect your clothing, you will be provided with a recyclable waterproof poncho.


    During the final part of the tour, you will be given a guided tour of the city where you’ll get to learn about the rich history as well as the distinct geography of Niagara Falls.

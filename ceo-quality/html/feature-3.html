    ITINERARY

    INFORMATION

    This adventure-filled tour of Niagara Falls on a helicopter starts with a direct pick-up from your hotel. You can enjoy the narrated tour of the entire city while heading up to the impressive falls.


    The first stop of the tour is the Journey Behind the Falls experience. During this part of the tour, you will descend around 125 feet and explore the cave-like tunnels that are located behind the falls. The tunnels lead to two observation decks from where you can snap amazing pictures of the falls, which are directly in front of you.


    The next experience of this tour is quite unforgettable – a helicopter ride above Niagara Falls! From the helicopter, you get to experience the real power and beauty of the falls and look out over the gorge and its surroundings. You will not only take some amazing pictures but you will make memories to last a lifetime.


    Once you have finished the helicopter ride, you will be taken on a Niagara Gorge cruise. This allows you to get up close to the main falls while embracing the natural beauty all around. By sailing through the Niagara Gorge, you can feel the power of the most majestic falls in the world.


    After the cruise, you will visit the Skylon Tower and have a hearty lunch at the restaurant. You will be taken to the restaurant area via a glass elevator overlooking the falls. The restaurant also offers full panoramic views of the falls allowing you to have a great time with friends and family.

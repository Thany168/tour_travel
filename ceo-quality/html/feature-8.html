    OVERVIEW

    Discover the best that Banff has to offer! The Canadian Rockies is an amazing place to explore. Its breathtaking scenery is sure to take your breath away or at least leave you speechless.

    Highlights

    Banff National Park

    Lake Louise

    Banff

    Short Hikes

    Canadian Rockies

    Lake Minnewanka

    Canmore

    This experience, from the moment you are picked up till the time you are brought back to your hotel, will take around 10-12 hours. You'll be part of a small group of 5 people so that you can enjoy the tour peacefully and with no rush.


    We'll stop at the main tourist attractions, such as Lake Minnewanka, the town of Banff, Canmore, Lake Louise, and many others. We'll have plenty of time to explore the short hiking trails and enjoy the scenery. We'll also have free time to shop and dine in the picturesque town of Banff.


    Come and join us on this incredible day trip adventure!
